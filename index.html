Frequently asked

Is HelpMeFind free to use?

Yes — there are no booking or service fees, ever. We simply connect you with the student or landlord, and you arrange the lease directly with them.

Who can list a place?

EHL students subletting or sharing their flat, plus local landlords and agencies. Student hosts get an "EHL verified" badge so you know who you're talking to.

Do I pay rent or my deposit through the site?

No. HelpMeFind only handles the introduction. Rent, the deposit and the contract are all arranged directly with the host — never through us.

What deposit should I expect?

In Switzerland it's typically around two months' rent, paid into a dedicated tenant deposit account and returned when you move out.

When should I start looking?

Most autumn-semester places appear from June onwards, and subleases pop up all year. Save a search and we'll keep it handy for when new ones land.

Can I find flatmates, not just a room?

Yes — filter by Flatshare to find rooms in shared student flats, and see your future flatmates' year and programme on each listing.
